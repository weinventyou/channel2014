ABOUT
Thirty Second Stories is an ongoing collaborative project. User-submitted stories are combined with audio and visuals and then collected into ten-story episodes. The playback of the stories within the episodes is randomized.
SUBMIT
If you would like to submit a story for the next episode, let me know you're interested at ZachRScott@gmail.com and I'll reply with details.
